HOW TO PLAY?
The objective is to land the rocket in a suitable speed, avoiding the crash
WARNING: The rocket's fuel is limited

MODES
EASY --> Maximum speed 5 km/h
MEDIUM --> Maximum speed 3 km/h
HARD --> Maximum speed 1 km/h

CONTROLS
SPACE --> Hold to motor ON
MOUSE CLICK --> Hold to motor ON
P --> Displays Pause
H --> Displays Help
R --> Restart the game
